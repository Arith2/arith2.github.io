Biography

Currently I am a first-year PhD student in Systems Group in ETH, supervised by Prof. Gustavo Alonso. I obtained my master degree in D-ITET from ETH Zurich in May 2022. I have finished my Bachelor study in Southeast University, China, in June 2019. My research interest focuses on computer systems, reconfigurable hardware and data management. Currently I am working on recommendation inference on FPGA clusters. Here is my CV.
